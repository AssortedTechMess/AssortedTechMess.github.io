Privacy Policy for Astronaut’s Lament

Last Updated: April 4, 2025

Astronaut’s Lament is a single-player 2D platformer developed by K. Wood Games. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you play Astronaut’s Lament through the Epic Games Store.

1. Information We Collect

Astronaut’s Lament does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The game uses `localStorage` on your device to save high scores (e.g., highest level reached, asteroids destroyed). This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to us or any third party.

2. Information Shared by Epic Games

When you play Astronaut’s Lament through the Epic Games Store, Epic Games shares limited account information with us to enable authentication and gameplay features. This may include your Epic Account ID and display name. We do not store or process this information beyond what is necessary to run the game.

3. Third-Party Services

Astronaut’s Lament does not use any third-party services that collect personal data. All gameplay occurs offline on your device.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share any personal information with third parties, as we do not collect any personal data.

5. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal information to access, delete, or manage. If you have questions about the data Epic Games collects, please refer to Epic’s Privacy Policy at https://www.epicgames.com/privacypolicy.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at assortedtechmess@gmail.com.
